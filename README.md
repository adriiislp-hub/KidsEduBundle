# Kids Educational Bundle

A curated collection of 9 educational PDF volumes for kids, organized with clickable Tables of Contents.

## How to use

Open `index.html` or visit the GitHub Pages URL to browse and download the volumes.

## Volumes

1. Alphabet Part 1 — Beginning Sounds, Cut & Paste
2. Alphabet Part 2 — Tracing, Flash Cards & More
3. Numbers, Tracing, Shapes & Coloring
4. Reading
5. Sight Words, Events & Flashcards
6. Pretend Play
7. Activities & Games
8. Charts, Calendars, Colors & Dot Activities
9. Emotions, Matching, Mazes, Patterns & More
